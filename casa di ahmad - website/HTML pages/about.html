A cozy Italian spot

Casa di Ahmad in Nottingham is an Italian restaurant which teaches the best delicious Italian food for the citizens and tourists of Nottingham, it is made by the Italian winner of top chef in the world in 2019 Ahmad Hamideh. It teaches the most delicious Italian food in the city of Nottingham, it also offers student discounts due to the high number of students in the city, the city of Nottingham does have two of the biggest universities in the United Kingdom. The restaurant will teach for the customers the highest quality of foods, coffee and desserts made by fine Italian products and made by a talented Italian chef who was born and raised in Italy in the city of Milan, all of the products will be shipped from Italy to the city of Nottingham freshly in Fridges daily such as the famous Italian tomato made in the Italian green fields and the famous parmesan cheese stored and dry aged in the best Italian containers for up to 5 years to give it the sharpy taste and melts in every customers mouth with its special cheese taste richness.
